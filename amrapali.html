🏆 Why Amrapali is Special

  • A high-yielding hybrid cross between Dashehari and Neelum.
  • Known for its dwarf stature, making it ideal for high-density planting.
  • Possesses a deep, vibrant orange pulp rich in beta-carotene.
  • Highly popular for both direct consumption and industrial processing.
  • Consistent bearer with a long shelf-life compared to native varieties.

📊 Expert Summary

Amrapali is a revolutionary hybrid variety that has transformed orchard productivity in Bihar. With its exceptionally high TSS levels and manageable tree size, it represents the intersection of scientific breeding and agricultural efficiency. It is a preferred choice for modern orchard management and sustained fruit availability.
