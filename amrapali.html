🏆 Why Amrapali is Special

  • A revolutionary hybrid variety developed specifically for high-density planting layouts.
  • Possesses a distinct, deep orange-colored pulp and an exceptionally sweet taste profile.
  • Regular and prolific bearer suitable for high-density orchard management systems.
  • Ideal for both smallholder home orchards and intensive commercial planting systems.
  • Demonstrates excellent yield efficiency and compact growth habit.

📊 Expert Summary

Amrapali is a notable hybrid developed from a cross between Dashehari and Neelam. It has transformed mango cultivation frameworks by enabling ultra-high-density planting due to its signature dwarf stature and regular bearing habit. Valued across Bihar for its elite TSS levels and deep orange pulp density, it represents modern, scientific horticulture scaling.

Amrapali is a hybrid mango variety developed from Dashehari and Neelam. It is known for its dwarf growth habit, regular bearing, deep orange pulp and high sweetness. The variety is widely adopted for high density planting systems and commercial orchards across Bihar and India. Amrapali represents modern scientific horticulture and is one of the most successful hybrid mango cultivars developed in India.
