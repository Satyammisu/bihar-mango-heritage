🏅 Popular High-Yielding Hybrid

🌟 Uniqueness

Amrapali is a highly successful hybrid mango variety developed by crossing Dashehari and Neelam. It is prized for its dwarf stature, allowing for high-density planting, and its regular bearing habit. It is famous for its high carotene content, giving it a vibrant deep orange pulp.

💡 Did You Know?


Because of its dwarf nature, Amrapali is a favorite among farmers for high-density orchards. It was specifically developed to provide a regular supply of high-quality mangoes, ensuring it is a top choice for commercial cultivation.
