🏆 Why Amrapali is Special

  • A revolutionary hybrid variety developed specifically for high-density planting.
  • Possesses a distinct, deep orange-colored pulp and an exceptionally sweet taste profile.
  • Regular and prolific bearer suitable for high-density orchard systems.
  • Ideal for both home orchards and commercial high-density commercial Origin.
  • Demonstrates excellent yield efficiency and compact growth habit.

📊 Expert Summary

Amrapali is a notable hybrid developed from a cross between Dashehari and Neelam. It has transformed mango Origin by enabling high-density planting due to its dwarf stature and regular bearing habit. Valued for its high TSS content and deep orange pulp, Amrapali is a staple in modern orchards, balancing excellent fruit quality with practical horticultural advantages.
