🏆 Why Bombai is Special

  • One of the most popular and earliest-ripening varieties in Bihar.
  • Highly valued for marking the beginning of the mango season.
  • Features a characteristic balanced flavor profile that appeals to a wide audience.
  • Maintains strong traditional significance within the regional market.
  • Reliable harvest timing makes it a staple for early-season supply chains.

📊 Expert Summary

Bombai is one of the oldest and most widely recognized traditional mango varieties cultivated in Bihar. Known for its early maturity, pleasant sweetness and balanced flavour profile, it marks the beginning of the mango season in many parts of the state. The variety is appreciated for its consistent yield, consumer acceptance and historical importance in Bihar's mango-growing landscape. Bombai continues to remain a preferred choice among growers and mango enthusiasts due to its dependable performance and traditional appeal.
