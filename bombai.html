🏆 Why Bombai is Special

  • One of the first mango varieties to arrive in the market.
  • Highly valued for its juicy texture and refreshing taste.
  • Traditional variety widely grown in Bihar orchards, especially in Sitamarhi.
  • Known for its ability to provide early-season income for farmers.
  • Attractive appearance with a distinct yellowish-green hue upon ripening.

📊 Expert Summary

Bombai represents the early-harvest segment of Bihar's mango industry. Its early arrival timing makes it a critical variety for market leadership and consumer satisfaction at the onset of the mango season. It offers a balanced taste profile that is highly anticipated by domestic consumers annually.

🩺 Nutritional Information

Bombai mango is a good source of vitamins, minerals and antioxidants.
