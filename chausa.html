🏆 Why Chausa is Special

  • Extends the mango marketing calendar significantly by dominant performance deep into July and August.
  • Universally acknowledged for hitting among the absolute highest natural TSS levels recorded in commercial lineages.
  • Historically backed by rich regional folklore, retaining massive consumer demand across both domestic and export channels.
  • Presents an uniform, fiberless bright yellow pulp structure that delivers an intensely rich, honeyed aftertaste.
  • Displays strong, enduring vegetative field vigor that withstands changing weather patterns.

📊 Expert Summary

Chausa serves as the definitive anchor for the late-season mango window across Bihar. Characterized by its brilliant golden-yellow skin upon ripening and an exceptionally low-fiber matrix, this variety ensures sustained economic returns to orchard owners well after mid-season varieties close production. It remains one of the most commercially important late-season mango cultivars cultivated across Bihar and northern India.
