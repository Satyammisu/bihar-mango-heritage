🏆 Why Chausa is Special

  • One of India's most celebrated mango varieties known for exceptional sweetness.
  • Famous for its unique, intense, and long-lasting aroma.
  • The flesh is remarkably soft, juicy, and nearly fiberless.
  • A late-season variety that marks the grand finale of the mango season.
  • Deeply rooted in the heritage of Buxar, Bihar.

📊 Expert Summary

Chausa is the jewel of Bihar's late-season mango portfolio. Its high sugar content and signature fragrance make it a celebrated dessert mango. As a premium variety from Buxar, it is vital for high-value market positioning and represents the pinnacle of Bihar's mango quality.
