🏆 Why Chausa is Special

  • Widely regarded as the sweetest and most aromatic mango variety in India.
  • Possesses a soft, succulent pulp with absolutely no fiber.
  • Known for its unique golden-yellow skin and rich, honey-like taste.
  • Highly valued for its late-season availability, extending the mango harvest period.
  • A signature variety that holds significant historical and culinary importance in the Buxar region of Bihar.

📊 Expert Summary

Chausa is a premier late-season variety that remains the pinnacle of flavor for mango connoisseurs. Originating from the fertile lands near Buxar, it is distinguished by its intense aroma, deep golden pulp, and exceptional sugar content. Its soft, fiberless texture and balanced nutritional profile make it a sought-after fruit in both domestic and export markets, marking a grand finale to the mango season in Bihar.
