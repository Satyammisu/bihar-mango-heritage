🌟 Uniqueness

Chausa Mango is one of the most popular mango varieties cultivated in Bihar. It is famous for its exceptionally sweet taste, juicy pulp and attractive golden-yellow appearance. The fruit is highly preferred for direct consumption and is loved across India for its rich flavour.

💡 Did You Know?


Chausa Mango originated in the historic region of Buxar and has gained recognition throughout India for its delicious taste and juicy texture. It remains one of the most demanded table mangoes during the summer season.
