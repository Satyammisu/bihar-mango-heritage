🩺 Health Insight for Diabetics

Our regional Chausa mango maintains a balanced Glycemic Index (GI), providing a controlled and slower release of natural sugars. When consumed in moderation, it serves as a scientifically preferable, nutrient-rich alternative for diabetic patients compared to high-TSS commercial hybrid mangoes found in other regions.
