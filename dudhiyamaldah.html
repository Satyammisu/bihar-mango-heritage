🏆 Why Dudhiya Malda is Special

  • Renowned for its signature "milky" (dudhiya) sweetness that is distinct to the Digha region.
  • Possesses a remarkably buttery and melt-in-the-mouth texture.
  • A historically significant variety that has served as a cultural symbol of Patna for generations.
  • Highly sought after for its refined eating quality and premium status.
  • Represents the legacy of the historic Digha mango orchards along the Ganges.

📊 Expert Summary

Dudhiya Malda is an iconic mango variety native to the Digha region of Patna, Bihar. It is widely celebrated for its delicate, milky sweetness and exceptionally smooth, buttery pulp. As a heritage cultivar, it represents the rich agricultural history of the riparian plains of the Ganges. Dudhiya Malda remains a benchmark for quality in Bihar's mango-growing landscape, maintaining high consumer demand due to its superior organoleptic properties.
