🏆 Why Dudhiya Maldah is Special

  • Renowned for its distinctive, creamy, 'milky' pulp texture.
  • A traditional favourite in Bihar households for its balanced sweetness.
  • Highly valued for its smooth, fiber-free consistency.
  • Features a subtle and sophisticated fragrance.
  • Represents the rich biodiversity of Bihar's mango orchards.

📊 Expert Summary

Dudhiya Maldah is one of Bihar's premium heritage mango varieties. With TSS values comparable to Alphonso and a moderate Glycemic Index, it offers an excellent balance of sweetness, flavour and nutritional value. The variety remains highly preferred for fresh consumption due to its smooth texture and rich taste profile.

🩺 Nutritional Information

Dudhiya Maldah mango contains natural fruit sugars, vitamins, minerals and antioxidants. The Glycemic Index (GI) and Total Soluble Solids (TSS) values presented here are intended for informational and educational purposes.
