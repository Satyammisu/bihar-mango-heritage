📌 Key Identification Features

  • Golden creamy-yellow pulp.
  • Exceptionally smooth and fiberless texture.
  • Distinct milky mouthfeel.
  • Uniform ripening characteristics.
  • Associated with the historic Digha mango belt of Patna.

🏆 Why Dudhiya Maldah is Special

  • The most celebrated heritage mango historically associated with Digha Ghat, Patna.
  • Famed for its unique "milky" (dudhiya) texture and consistency.
  • Exceptionally smooth and fiberless pulp that melts on the palate.
  • Highly valued for its sweet, non-acidic flavor profile.

📊 Expert Summary

Dudhiya Maldah is an iconic mango of Patna, with deep historical roots in the Digha Ghat region. Celebrated for its characteristic creamy, milk-like pulp texture and lack of fiber, it is a hallmark of local mango diversity. This heritage variety continues to be a symbol of Digha’s rich horticultural legacy and remains a seasonal favorite for connoisseurs.
