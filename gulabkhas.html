🏆 Why Gulabkhas is Special

  • Renowned for its unique aroma that hints at the fragrance of a rose.
  • Distinct, attractive golden-yellow skin with a sophisticated red blush upon ripening.
  • Highly valued in Bihar as a premium, aromatic table mango.
  • Offers a sophisticated taste profile that is distinct from standard sweet varieties.
  • Represents the rich horticultural heritage of the Bhagalpur and Eastern Bihar region.

📊 Expert Summary

Gulabkhas is among Bihar's most distinctive heritage mango varieties, celebrated for its characteristic floral fragrance and balanced sweetness. Its unique aroma profile differentiates it from most commercial mango cultivars and makes it a valued specialty fruit within Bihar's traditional mango landscape.
