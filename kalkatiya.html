🏅 Traditional Heritage Variety

🌟 Uniqueness

Kalkatiya is a well-known traditional mango variety in Bihar, cherished for its distinct sweet flavour and aromatic pulp. It remains a reliable and popular choice among consumers who value the authentic taste of classic regional mangoes during the peak harvest season.
