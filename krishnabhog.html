🏆 Why Kalkatiya is Essential

  • Widely preferred for traditional oil-based pickle preparation in the Darbhanga region, Bihar.
  • Its firm, dense, and moderately fibrous pulp ensures structural integrity during sun-drying and processing.
  • Remarkably durable, making it ideal for long-term preservation in household kitchens.
  • Represents the culinary backbone of the Darbhanga region's traditional mango heritage.
  • Versatile in culinary use for chutney, pickles, and traditional beverages like Aam Panna.

📊 Expert Summary

Kalkatiya occupies a unique position within Bihar's mango diversity as a specialized processing and pickling cultivar. Its firm texture, high keeping quality, and suitability for traditional preservation techniques have made it an integral component of North Bihar's culinary heritage for generations.

🩺 Nutritional Information

Kalkatiya mango contains natural carbohydrates, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. The information presented here is intended for educational purposes. Consumption patterns may vary depending on preparation methods such as pickling, chutney making, or fresh culinary use.
