🏆 Why Langra is Special

  • Features a characteristic green skin that persists even when the fruit is fully ripe.
  • Prized for its unique, rich, and slightly spicy sweetness.
  • Boasts a smooth, fiber-free pulp texture that is highly valued by consumers.
  • A cornerstone of the traditional mango heritage in the Ganges plains of Bihar.
  • Recognized as one of the most reliable and flavorful varieties for home and commercial orchards.

📊 Expert Summary

Langra is a legendary mango variety that maintains its green hue throughout the ripening process, making it immediately recognizable. Its zestful, intense flavor profile and firm, fiber-free texture have secured its status as a staple variety across Bihar. Widely cultivated in the fertile plains along the Ganges, Langra remains a favorite among mango enthusiasts for its consistent eating quality and traditional appeal.
