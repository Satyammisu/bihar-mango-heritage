📊 Expert Summary

Krishnabhog is a well-regarded heritage table mango of Bihar, valued for its smooth texture, pleasant sweetness, and consistent fruit quality. Its low fiber content and attractive pulp characteristics make it a preferred choice for fresh consumption and an important component of Bihar's traditional mango diversity.
