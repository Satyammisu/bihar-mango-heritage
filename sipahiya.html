🏆 Why Sipahiya is Special

  • Sipahiya serves as the welcome sign for the mango season in Bihar.
  • Traditionally associated with mango orchards of Samastipur and the Mithila region.
  • Its early harvest allows it to command significant market demand.
  • A reliable table variety that bridges the gap before the main-season harvest.

📊 Expert Summary

Sipahiya is a traditional mango variety associated with Samastipur and the wider Mithila region of Bihar. Recognized as one of the earliest mangoes to enter local markets, it plays an important role in marking the beginning of the state's mango season. Its balanced sweetness, pleasant flavor, and reliable productivity have made it a favored table variety among growers and consumers across North Bihar.
