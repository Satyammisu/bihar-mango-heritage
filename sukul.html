🏆 Why Sukul is Special

  • A cherished local variety known for its pleasant taste profile.
  • Traditionally associated with mango orchards of Samastipur and the Mithila region.
  • Highly valued in regional markets for its distinct appearance and quality.
  • Known for having smooth, low-fiber pulp, making it excellent for fresh table use.
  • Contributes to the rich diversity of Bihar’s mango germplasm.

📊 Expert Summary

Sukul is a traditional mango variety associated with the Mithila region of Bihar, particularly the districts of Darbhanga, Samastipur and Vaishali. Appreciated for its balanced sweetness, smooth texture and pleasant flavour, the variety occupies a special place in local orchards and seasonal markets. Its continued cultivation reflects the rich mango heritage and biodiversity of North Bihar.
